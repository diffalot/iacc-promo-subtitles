This is a remix of an Internet Archive and Creative Commons promotional video that may be found here.

This remix adds one Motion file and a Final Cut Project to the project folder. Start out with the sparseimage from the IACC-Promo, add these two files, and you'll have a full copy of all raw materials should you wish to remix it yourself. Here is the archive.org page with this projects raw materials.

Please let me know if you remix it, I'd love to see what you've done.
